## Introduction

There are two types of stepper: _linear_ and _circular_.

## Linear Stepper

**Linear stepper** displays progress of a particular context in steps and in linear fashion.

```html preview=true
<div class="LinearStepper">
  <div class="LinearStepper-step is-done">
    <div class="LinearStepper-stepStatus">1</div>
    <div class="LinearStepper-stepDescription">
      <p class="LinearStepper-stepTitle">Step 1</p>
      <p class="LinearStepper-stepSubtitle">Step description</p>
    </div>
  </div>
  <div class="LinearStepper-step is-done">
    <div class="LinearStepper-stepStatus">2</div>
    <div class="LinearStepper-stepDescription">
      <p class="LinearStepper-stepTitle">Step 2</p>
      <p class="LinearStepper-stepSubtitle">Step description</p>
    </div>
  </div>
  <div class="LinearStepper-step is-active">
    <div class="LinearStepper-stepStatus">3</div>
    <div class="LinearStepper-stepDescription">
      <p class="LinearStepper-stepTitle">Step 3</p>
      <p class="LinearStepper-stepSubtitle">Step description</p>
    </div>
  </div>
  <div class="LinearStepper-step">
    <div class="LinearStepper-stepStatus">4</div>
    <div class="LinearStepper-stepDescription">
      <p class="LinearStepper-stepTitle">Step 4</p>
      <p class="LinearStepper-stepSubtitle">Step description</p>
    </div>
  </div>
  <div class="LinearStepper-step">
    <div class="LinearStepper-stepStatus">5</div>
    <div class="LinearStepper-stepDescription">
      <p class="LinearStepper-stepTitle">Step 5</p>
      <p class="LinearStepper-stepSubtitle">Step description</p>
    </div>
  </div>
</div>
```

## Circular Stepper

**Circular stepper** shows progress in a more simplified and compact form.

```html preview=true
<div class="CircularStepper">
  <div class="CircularStepper-ring">
    <div class="CircularStepper-indicator">3/5</div>
  </div>
  <div class="CircularStepper-description">
    <div class="CircularStepper-title">
      Stepper
    </div>
    <div class="CircularStepper-subtitle">
      Circular Stepper
    </div>
  </div>
</div>
```
